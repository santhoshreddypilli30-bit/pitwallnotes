About this journal

Trackside Ink is a personal motorsport writing project by Santhosh Reddy Pilli, a Mechanical Engineering student from RGUKT Nuzvid with a focus on telemetry analysis and vehicle dynamics. Each article works through the engineering and strategy behind a race weekend — tyre physics, pit windows, and the small decisions that decide championships.
