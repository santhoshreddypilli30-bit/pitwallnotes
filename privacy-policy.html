Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Pit Wall Notes ("this site", "we", "us") is a personal motorsport analysis journal written and published by Santhosh Reddy Pilli. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit pitwallnotes.online and how it's used.

Information we collect

This site does not require you to create an account, log in, or submit personal information to read any article. We do not run our own tracking scripts or collect personally identifiable information directly.

If you use the contact details on our Contact page to reach out by email, we'll have whatever information you choose to include in that message (your email address, name, and message content). That information is used only to respond to you and is not shared with third parties.

Cookies and third-party services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts and images from Unsplash, both of which may set cookies or log requests (such as IP address and browser type) as part of serving that content, under their own respective privacy policies.

If and when this site displays advertising through Google AdSense, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, and by visiting www.aboutads.info for other providers' opt-outs.

This site may also use Google Analytics or Google Search Console to understand aggregate traffic patterns (which pages are read, roughly how many visitors, which countries traffic comes from) — this data is anonymized and aggregated, and is not linked back to your identity.

Your choices

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect how some embedded content (like fonts or ads) displays, but will not prevent you from reading any article on this site.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time as the site grows — for example, if advertising or analytics tools are added. The "last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the Contact page.
